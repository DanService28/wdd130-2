The best time to visit the Galapagos Islands depends on your preferences. If you prefer warmer weather and calm seas, the warm/wet season may be more suitable. If you are interested in observing the unique behaviors of wildlife during different times of the year, you might choose the cool/dry season. However, keep in mind that the Galapagos Islands are a year-round destination with an incredible variety of wildlife and landscapes to offer, regardless of the season. You can travel during either of the two seasons, as both provide the opportunity to witness diverse attractions and unique animals.
1. Warm/Wet Season (December to May)
2. Cool/Dry Season (June to November)

The Galápagos Islands feature a range of popular destinations beyond Santa Cruz, Isabela, and San Cristóbal. Islands like Bartolomé, Pinzón, Daphne, and others offer unique experiences. Despite some being more renowned, each island on our tours provides a distinctive and captivating encounter, showcasing the incredible diversity of the Galápagos.
